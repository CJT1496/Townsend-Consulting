About Townsend Consulting

Townsend Consulting, Inc. (TCI) is a corporate search firm specializing in providing Information Technology and Engineering solutions for management challenges faced by client companies. The company provides a suite of executive search and contract services.

TCI's focus is to seek out, recruit and secure the best and brightest talent with proven performance within their respected organizations. We work with companies and organizations that understand that their talent is their differentiator and they enlist our help to take that talent and management practices to the next level. Founded in 1999, TCI is a privately held company based in Atlanta, Georgia.
